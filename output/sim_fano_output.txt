======================================================================
  TEST 3: ANALYTIC LARGE-r_p LIMIT
======================================================================

  For the stars-and-bars entropy on a spherical shell:
    N_horizon ~ 4*pi*r_p^2 (continuum limit)
    E_total ~ N_horizon * phi_max (all nodes at phi_max for transition shell)
    S_unc = gammaln(E+N) - gammaln(E+1) - gammaln(N)

  For E/N = phi_max = const, Stirling gives:
    S_unc/N ~ (1 + phi_max)*ln(1 + phi_max) - phi_max*ln(phi_max)
    = (1 + 3.934)*ln(1 + 3.934) - 3.934*ln(3.934)
    = 2.487154

  S_unc/(A/4) = S_unc / (pi*r_p^2)
             = N_horizon * s_per_n / (pi*r_p^2)
             = 4*pi*r_p^2 * s_per_n / (pi*r_p^2)
             = 4 * s_per_n
             = 4 * 2.487154
             = 9.948617

  ANALYTIC RESULT: S_unc/(A/4) -> 9.9486 in continuum limit
  Distance from 7.000: 2.9486
  Distance from 7.300: 2.6486
